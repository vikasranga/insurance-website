Frequently Asked Questions About Insurance

Get answers to common insurance queries

What is insurance?

Insurance is a financial product that protects you against unexpected losses. You pay a premium, and the insurer covers your risks as per the policy terms.

Why do I need insurance?

Insurance helps you manage risks and avoid financial burdens from emergencies. It offers financial security to you and your family.

What types of insurance are there?

Common types include Health, Life, Motor, Travel, Home, and Business insurance. Each type serves a specific protection need.

What is a premium?

A premium is the amount you pay regularly to keep your insurance active. It can be monthly, quarterly, or annually depending on your plan.

How do I file a claim?

Contact your insurer or agent, submit necessary documents, and follow the claim process.
