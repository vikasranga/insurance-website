Cyber Insurance

Data breach, ransomware & digital risk cover

Cyber Insurance provides essential protection for businesses against digital threats such as data breaches, cyberattacks, ransomware, phishing, and system outages. In today's technology-driven environment, even a minor cyber incident can lead to financial loss, data compromise, business interruption, and reputational damage. Cyber Insurance helps organisations safeguard sensitive information, secure digital operations, and recover quickly from cyber incidents. Benefits of Cyber Insurance

Cyber Insurance offers comprehensive coverage for expenses related to data restoration, system recovery, legal liabilities, breach notifications, and cyber extortion. It also helps businesses manage operational disruption by covering income loss during downtime. Many policies extend to forensic investigations, PR support to manage reputational impact, and liability protection against claims from customers or partners affected by a breach. With Cyber Insurance, businesses strengthen their digital resilience, improve compliance, and ensure continuity even when facing complex cyber threats.
