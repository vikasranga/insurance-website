Trade Credit Insurance

Receivables, business non-payment risk

Trade Credit Insurance protects businesses against the risk of non-payment from buyers due to insolvency, protracted default, or political disruptions. Companies that sell goods or services on credit often face uncertainty in recovering payments, especially when dealing with new clients, higher volumes, or international markets. Trade Credit Insurance ensures that outstanding invoices are safeguarded, enabling businesses to grow confidently without the fear of bad debts. It also supports stronger credit management by providing financial stability, improved cash flow, and greater confidence in extending credit to customers.

Benefits of Trade Credit Insurance

Trade Credit Insurance offers robust financial protection by covering unpaid invoices resulting from buyer insolvency, delayed payment, or bankruptcy. It strengthens cash flow by ensuring that receivables remain secure even during market uncertainties. Businesses also gain access to credit risk insights, helping them evaluate the financial strength of potential buyers. With enhanced credit control, improved financing opportunities, and protection against domestic and export risk, Trade Credit Insurance becomes a vital tool for sustaining growth and reducing financial exposure.
