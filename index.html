About Rishabh Insurance Brokers Pvt Ltd

Rishabh Insurance Brokers Pvt Ltd is a trusted name in the Insurance Industry, offering expert and reliable insurance solutions across India. We are an IRDAI Licensed Direct (General) Insurance Broker with Registration No. 578.

Our leadership team and directors have previously worked with India's top PSU Insurance Companies. With cumulative experience of over 100 years in General Insurance, their deep understanding of the insurance market, Risk Analysis, Client Understanding, claim processes allows us to offer unmatched service quality and practical solutions to every client.

Established over 10 years ago, we have built Reputation on Trust, Customized Transparency and Service excellence. We believe that every client deserves a plan that fits their specific needs — which is why we provide Tailor-made Insurance Solutions designed to protect what matters most, at the most suitable rate.

Our expertise covers a wide range of General Insurance services, including Home Insurance, Travel Insurance, Corporate Insurance, Fire & Marine Insurance, Liability Insurance, Credit Insurance, Surety Insurance and other line of businesses. Each product is carefully selected and customized to suit the individual or business requirements of our clients.

We understand that insurance is not only about buying a policy but also about standing by our clients when they need us the most. To make this possible, we have an in-house claim management team that ensures Smooth, Fast and Fair claim settlements. Our dedicated claim consultancy team assists clients at every step as from documentation and follow-up to final claim realization saving both time and stress.

At Rishabh Insurance Brokers Pvt Ltd, we aim to simplify the insurance experience by combining industry expertise with personalized service. We work with India's top insurance companies, giving our clients access to a wide range of options and ensuring they always receive the most suitable policy at the most competitive rate.

We believe that insurance should give Confidence not Confusion. That's why we take the time to understand every client's needs, explain policies in simple terms, and recommend solutions that truly add value.
