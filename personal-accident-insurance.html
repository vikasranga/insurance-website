Personal Accident Cover

Accident, disability, and death cover

Personal Accident Cover provides financial protection in case of accidental death, disability, or injury. Accidents can happen anytime—while travelling, working, or even at home—and the impact can be both emotional and financial. This coverage ensures that you and your family remain protected from sudden income loss, medical expenses, and long-term recovery costs arising due to an accident. Whether the injury is minor or severe, Personal Accident Insurance offers crucial financial support during challenging times and helps maintain stability and security for your loved ones.

Benefits of Personal Accident Cover

Personal Accident Cover offers a strong safety net by providing immediate financial assistance in case of accidental events. It includes compensation for accidental death, permanent disability, temporary disability, and covers injury-related medical expenses. This ensures that even if income stops due to an accident, financial stability continues. Many plans also offer education benefits for children, transportation expenses, hospital cash, and additional support for rehabilitation. With easy documentation, worldwide coverage, and 24/7 protection, Personal Accident Insurance guarantees peace of mind wherever you go.
