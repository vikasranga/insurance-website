Transit Insurance

Coverage for goods in transit, marine/cargo risk

Transit Insurance provides financial protection for goods while they are being transported from one location to another by road, rail, air, or sea. Whether you are a manufacturer, trader, distributor, logistics operator, or small business owner, your goods are exposed to risks such as theft, damage, accidents, fire, or natural calamities during transit. Transit Insurance ensures that products remain safeguarded throughout the journey, whether they are being moved within a city or across the country. With the increasing movement of raw materials and finished goods in today's supply chain, Transit Insurance helps businesses maintain continuity, reduce losses, and deliver products safely and securely.

Benefits of Transit Insurance

Transit Insurance offers comprehensive protection from the moment goods leave the warehouse until they reach their destination. It covers loss or damage caused by accidents, fire, explosion, mishandling, overturning, or theft of the transporting vehicle. Many policies also include coverage for loading and unloading, short-term storage, and temporary halts during the journey. This insurance safeguards the financial value of the shipment, reduces business disruption, and ensures smoother logistics operations. With flexible options and wide applicability, it becomes an essential solution for anyone moving goods locally or nationally.
