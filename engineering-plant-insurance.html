Engineering/Plant Insurance

Machinery, construction, contractor's all-risk

Engineering/Plant Insurance provides comprehensive protection for businesses that rely on mechanical, electrical, and industrial equipment. Industries such as construction, manufacturing, infrastructure, fabrication, and heavy engineering are exposed to risks like machinery breakdown, accidental damage, structural collapse, and equipment failure. This insurance offers financial protection against unforeseen losses that impact productivity, asset value, and project timelines. With coverage for both fixed and mobile equipment—from boilers and pressure plants to cranes, excavators, and production machinery—Engineering/Plant Insurance ensures continuity of operations, reduces downtime, and safeguards businesses from expensive repair and replacement costs.

Benefits of Engineering/Plant Insurance

Engineering/Plant Insurance offers wide coverage against operational and accidental risks. It protects machinery and equipment from sudden breakdowns, mechanical failures, fire, explosions, and damage during use or maintenance. It also covers civil engineering projects, contractor equipment, and third-party liabilities arising from accidental incidents. This insurance helps businesses maintain uninterrupted operations by reducing financial burden, supporting faster recovery, and ensuring compliance with safety and project requirements. With flexible plans and optional add-ons, Engineering/Plant Insurance becomes a critical risk-management tool for companies handling heavy machinery or complex engineering assets.
