Travel Insurance

Trip, medical, and baggage cover worldwide

Travel Insurance protects you from unexpected risks while travelling abroad or within the country. From medical emergencies and flight delays to baggage loss and passport issues, even a small incident can disrupt your entire trip and cause major financial stress. Travel Insurance ensures that you can explore, work, or study confidently without worrying about sudden expenses. Whether it's an international vacation, business trip, student travel, or corporate travel requirement, this coverage provides medical support, emergency assistance, trip protection, and peace of mind throughout your journey.

Benefits of Travel Insurance

Travel Insurance offers complete protection during your trip by covering medical emergencies, hospitalization, personal accidents, and evacuation requirements. It also safeguards you from non-medical issues like flight cancellations, delays, missed connections, and loss of baggage or passport. Many plans include cashless treatment abroad, 24/7 global assistance, and coverage for trip interruptions due to unforeseen events. With Travel Insurance, you stay financially secure, protected from unexpected losses, and free to enjoy your journey without hassles.
